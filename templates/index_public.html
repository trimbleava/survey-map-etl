Welcome to the Survey Map Preparation and GeoServer Readiness


Heath consultants performs multiple type of surveys, primarily aimed at the utility companies which distribute cooking and home heating methane / ethane gas to customer residences through underground pipelines. Heath offers two different services to utility companies:

  1. Leak detection equipment (Laser and Infrared based) sales, support, refurbishment, and repairs through its Process Business Unit (PBU)
  2. Technicians and crew who can use such equipment and perform survey on ground for leak detection on behalf of the customer through its Service Business Unit (SBU).

To conduct a successful survey the personnel need maps that show various assets like main and service pipeslines, proposed pipelines, high pressure pipelines, residential addresses, right of way, risers, stubs etc. overlayed on top of a street or satellite maps.
This interactive application amis at creation such maps (a.k.a pristine map), per customer through its ETL processing engine. Authorized internal developers may access the source code at: link to the source code. An example map is shown on the right.
